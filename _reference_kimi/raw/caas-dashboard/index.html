Executive Summary

The CAAsXplorer project represents a comprehensive institutional mapping initiative that transforms dispersed, regionally-specific service information from Brazil's 27 Lawyer Assistance Funds into a standardized, analytically rigorous framework.

Key Achievement

Classification of over 5,000 social media posts into 30+ standardized service categories, enabling meaningful comparison across autonomous regional sections.

Critical Correction

CAAMT's active domain identified as caamt.com.br, not caamt.org.br as previously documented— a crucial update for institutional connectivity.
